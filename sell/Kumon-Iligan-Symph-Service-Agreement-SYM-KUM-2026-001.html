Executive Summary

Symph has built and deployed the Kumon Iligan Operations Management System (Kumon Iligan OMS) for Mrs. Nina Quijano's Kumon franchise in Iligan City. The platform is live in production at kumon-iligan.symph.co and covers the full payment verification workflow agreed during the April 3, 2026 discovery call: teacher payment submission, admin review and flagging, receipt management, role-based access control, visual dashboards, data import/export, student and family management, and a complete audit trail.

Figures are based on the scope agreed during discovery and reflect the platform as delivered. This document becomes a binding agreement upon signature by authorized representatives of both parties.

§ 02

Problem Statement

Manual Transcription Bottleneck

Parents photograph receipts and send them to teachers. Teachers forward by email to admin. Admin manually re-enters every payment into a spreadsheet. With 15-20 teachers and hundreds of students, admin availability determines whether payment records are current.

No Structured Payment Visibility

Spreadsheets cannot surface which students are overdue, which teachers have pending submissions, or whether a record is complete. Identifying irregularities requires manual row-by-row review, making month-end reconciliation a multi-hour effort.

No Verification or Flag Workflow

When a submitted payment looked wrong, there was no structured way to flag it, notify the teacher, and track resolution. Disputes lived in email or chat threads that were easy to lose and impossible to audit later.

Multi-Sibling Payment Complexity

Many Kumon families enroll multiple siblings with different payment amounts and periods. The spreadsheet treated each student in isolation, making it difficult to track whether a family's full monthly obligation had been settled.

No Role Separation

Teachers, admins, and the franchise owner accessed the same spreadsheet with no differentiated views or permission controls. No mechanism existed to restrict sensitive records or enforce accountability by role.

Mobile-Unfriendly Process

Teachers are typically on their phones when handling payment receipts during class. A spreadsheet-based workflow on mobile required screenshot, app-switch, manual entry, and attachment: a multi-step process prone to delays and errors.

Proposed Solution

Kumon Iligan OMS is a mobile-first operations management platform that replaces the manual receipt-and-spreadsheet workflow with a structured digital system accessible to all roles from any device. The core design principle: structured data at the point of capture, not after the fact.

Submits payment via SDC form on mobile with receipt photo
2
Admin Review
Reviews in Payments table, verifies or flags the submission
3
Teacher Reply
Responds to flag in Recordings view without admin reopening
4
Verified Record
Payment confirmed, audit log entry created
5
Analytics
Dashboards updated: payer trends, overdue detection, teacher activity

Core Capabilities

Mobile-first payment submission with receipt upload
Admin verify, flag, and reject workflow
Teacher reply flow for flagged submissions
Visual dashboards: payer trends, method mix, teacher activity
Family and student records with multi-sibling support
Payment period tracking with overdue detection
Three-tier RBAC: Teacher, Admin, Superadmin
Spreadsheet import/export for migration and backup
Full audit trail for every system action
Multi-branch architecture ready for franchise expansion

§ 04

Scope of Work by Module

Data Accuracy Note

Kumon Iligan OMS captures what teachers and admins log. The system does not have independent payment sensors or bank integrations. If a payment is not submitted through the SDC form, it will not appear in the system. Data completeness depends on consistent use by teachers. This is a digitization tool, not a payment verification layer.

1

Teacher Payment Recording (SDC Form)

Mobile-first form at /sdc/[studentId] for teachers to submit payment details per student. Designed for single-handed use on a phone.

Student lookup by ID or name
Payment amount and reference number input
Receipt photo attachment via Supabase storage
Teacher's own submission history
2

Admin Payment Management

Spreadsheet-style admin view at /payments showing all submitted payments with status badges, quick filters, and receipt access.

Full payment table with mobile card fallback
Verify, flag, or reject any submission
View attached receipt photo per record
URL-synced filters, search, and month/year selector
3

Teacher Reply Flow

Flagged payments surface in the teacher's Recordings view (/recordings) with a sidebar notification badge refreshing every 30 seconds.

Flagged payment triage view for teachers
Sidebar badge showing pending flags
Reply and clarification per flagged submission
Admin sees teacher responses in payment detail
4

Visual Analytics & Reports

Dashboard at /reports with four real-time charts powered by live payment data.

Payer trend chart: monthly collection volume
Payment method mix breakdown
Recent payments activity feed
Teacher activity: submissions per teacher per period

Data Import & Export

Spreadsheet import for transitioning existing records; CSV export for accounting and reconciliation.

Payment import via CSV with row-level validation
Student import via CSV
Export all payments to CSV from /payments

Role-Based Access Control

Three-tier permission model enforced at both API (NestJS guards) and database (Row Level Security) levels.

Google OAuth sign-in (no password management required)

Student, Family & Period Management

Families and students managed as separate records with multi-sibling assignment. Payment periods track per-student billing cycles.

Family records with linked student rosters
Multi-sibling: one family, multiple student records
Payment period generation per student per month
Overdue detection via period vs. payment matching
8

Audit Trail

Every create, update, and delete action writes an immutable audit log entry. Admin-accessible at /audit with full change history.

Full audit log for all system actions
User, timestamp, entity, and action per entry
Admin viewer with filtering
Soft deletes: records never hard-deleted from the database
Open Item 1: Notification Wiring

The platform includes email and SMS service modules that are fully scaffolded but not yet connected to any trigger. If Nina wants automatic email notifications to teachers when admin flags a submission, that is a 30-minute wire-up available as a feature request.

Open Item 2: Tuition Rate Configuration

Currently, expected payment amounts are set per-student per-period. If Nina wants a centralized rate-per-Kumon-level configuration (e.g., all Level 1 students auto-assigned the same monthly rate), this requires a new rate configuration module and is available as a feature request.

Explicitly Out of Scope

  • Attendance tracking and teacher clock-in/out
  • Payroll processing or integration
  • Student progress tracking (Kumon level advancement and stagnation alerts)
  • Cross-branch or franchise-wide analytics (Iligan branch only for now)
  • Parent portal (explicitly excluded by client at discovery)

§ 08

Post-Launch Support & Feature Requests

Active subscription includes ongoing maintenance, security patches, cloud hosting, and email support with a 5-day SLA target. All subscription tiers also include 16 feature requests per year on Kumon Iligan OMS.

What Counts as a Feature Request

Subscription includes 16 feature requests per year on Kumon Iligan OMS: new screens, workflows, fields, roles, filters, or logic within existing modules. Unused requests do not carry over. One feature request equals one scoped deliverable. New modules and wholly separate systems are priced as separate engagements.

Counts as a Feature Request
  • New screen or view inside an existing module
  • New workflow step or approval stage
  • New filter, sort, or export option
  • New user role or permission
  • New notification trigger or rule
  • UI/UX redesign of an existing screen
  • New field, form, or data capture point
Does Not Count
  • Bug fixes: if it doesn't work as agreed in the PRD, Symph fixes it at no cost, unlimited
  • Security patches and performance improvements
  • Hosting, uptime, and infrastructure maintenance
  • PRD scope items: all items in the signed PRD are included regardless of volume
  • New modules (e.g., attendance, payroll): priced separately
  • A wholly new system: priced as a separate engagement
Feature requests are logged and prioritized in monthly planning sessions with Nina's team. The 16-per-year allowance resets at each contract anniversary. The two Open Items in § 04 (notification wiring and tuition rate config) are available as feature requests at any time.
